Our services

We are passionate about open & linked data

Ontology/Vocabulary Design

In the semantic web the terms ontology and vocabulary are both used to refer to documents describing concepts and the relationship between them. Practically, it means minting resolveable webaddresses for given concept. We offer assistance in selecting, creating and/or hosting of your ontology/vocabulary

Create RDF

Micelio has extensive experience in transforming almost any existing data format in to RDF, a common use data format in the Semantic web. Micelio offers both courses in RDF conversions as well as the development over conversion scripts.

SPARQL endpoint

All of Micelio's sparql endpoints are hosted on various cloud platforms. We can assist in launching your own SPARQL endpoint. Most of our SPARQL endppints are hosted on a Dutch public open stack cloud (fuga.io). Others run on Amazon or Digital Ocean. Contact us for details, if you want to host a SPARQL endpoint with us.

Topic parties

To answer question or gain insight, collecting, linking data and developing a technical infrastructure is only part of the sollution. The human factor is often forgotten. Micelio offers so-called topic parties, which are workshop driven by a problem, disease, or simply an interesting question. In a superb inspirational location we gather the people needed to solve a data driven problem. These problems can be anything from a disease to a set of observations or simply a basic question. You provide your problem, the data and we organise a inspirational session aimed at question answering.
